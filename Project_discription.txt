Project Name: whatsapp bot for saloon appointment booking
It was a simple whatsapp bot connected to our server programed in python 
user flow:
user can open our bot in his whatsapp
our bot need to check his location and search for nearest saloons in our database and display them for his selection 
next he need to select one saloon and get saloon services list for selection 
after selecting service he need to select date and time for appointment
his appointment need to be updated in database
